Citi ID- Urban Site Analysis and Design

One of the courses I took abroad in Rome, Italy was Urban Site Analysis and Design. We were all assigned a site and our task was to create a design intervention. Although the expectation was to design an architecture project, after conducting the site analysis it was clear an architecture project was not right for the space. Instead, I discovered the app Citi ID would be more suitable for the site's needs.
